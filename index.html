About us

Bee Tempa Ray Electrical Services is a professional electrical services company committed to providing safe, reliable, and cost-effective electrical solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Established in 2017, our mission is to deliver top-tier electrical work that meets the highest safety standards while exceeding client expectations.

What we do?

We offer a complete range of electrical services — from new installations to repairs, upgrades, and maintenance — ensuring clients receive prompt service and expert craftsmanship. Our certified electricians bring years of hands-on experience, ensuring projects are completed on time, within budget, and in compliance with industry regulations.

Our goal is not just to solve problems but to build lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service.
With growing demand and our dedication to quality and safety, Bee Tempa Ray Electrical Services is positioned for long-term success. We invite you to partner with us in powering the future of our community.

Our Services

Electrical Installations

We manage entire electrical installation projects for factories, warehouses, commercial offices, and residential properties across South Africa. Our services include everything from planning to execution, ensuring efficient, safe, and code-compliant installations.

Panel Design and Installation

We specialize in designing and installing Motor Control Centres (MCCs), Distribution Boards (DBs), and PLC Panels. Our panels are tailored to meet industrial, mining, and commercial electrical needs.

Data Cabling and Networking

Aspects Electrical installs advanced data cabling systems and networking infrastructure, ensuring reliable connectivity for businesses, data centers, and institutions.

Backup Power Systems (Generators and UPS)

We provide backup power systems, including diesel generators and UPS installations, ensuring uninterrupted power supply for essential operations across South Africa.

Solar and Renewable Energy

Our renewable energy services include the design and installation of solar power systems, wind energy solutions, and hybrid energy setups for residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Lighting Design and Installation

We design and install energy-efficient indoor and outdoor lighting systems for commercial spaces, industrial complexes, and residential properties.
